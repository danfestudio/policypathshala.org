  • 2018

    Public Policy Dialogue on Local Health Governance in Gandaki Province (2018)

    A multi-sector policy dialogue on issues of local health governance was organized in Pokhara on March 2018. Officials from over 40 local governments, Regional Director for Health (rechristened Provincial Health Directorate), district level health officials (rechristened Provincial health Office) and health sector experts actively participated in the province. The event focused on the challenges of federal transition in health sector.

  • 2019

    Public Policy Dialogue on Newborn Health in Province 2

    A public policy dialogue at provincial level was organized in Janakpur on 6 March, 2019 to share and discuss on the findings from series of discussion with the local governments at province 2 on newborn health.   • 2019

    Launch of the report on The Lancet Global Health Commission on High Quality Health Systems in the SDG Era

    PPP and Ministry of Health and Population (MoHP) of Nepal hosted the lunch of the report on The Lancet Global Health Commission on High Quality Health Systems in the SDG Era on 6 June, 2019 in Kathmandu, Nepal.   • 2019

    Public Policy Dialogue on “Procurement Reform in Health Sector”

    A Public Policy Dialogue on “Procurement Reform in Health Sector” was organized on 1 August, 20019 at Hotel Shangrila, Kathmandu. The dialogue was co- hosted by Public Policy Pathshala and The Asia Foundation to discuss on the Public Procurement Act and its implementation challenges in procurement of medicines and equipment’s. The dialogue was attended by health procurement experts, members of parliament, experts on local governance, donor representatives, civil society members, and other stakeholders.

  • 2019

    Social Movement for Quality Health in Nepal

    Public Policy Dialogue was held jointly organized by Public Policy Pathshala and Lancet Global Health Commission under the theme of Social Campaign for Quality Health. The dialogue was attended by leading personalities in the field of Nepal's political, social and public health, including Margaret Kirk, an associate professor at Harvard University, Krishna Pahadi, a human rights activist, Binda Pandey Parliamentary member, and Dr. Aruna Uprety public health expert.

  • 2016

    Haliya research

    In collaboration with Mukta Haliya Mahasangh and Agriculture and Water Resources Committee, the team of Mukta Haliya Mahasangh and Public Policy Pathshala conducted field study and interaction in Jajarkot, Surkhet, Darchula, Baitadi, Doti and Dadeldhura.   • 2015

    Land reform in Nepal : Six decades of debates

    An interaction program was held in Kathmandu, Kailali, Doti and Jhapa between the political leadership, journalists, land rights activists and stakeholders on the topic of six decades of land reform debates in Nepal. During the interaction and on-site visit, thinker Pradip Giri, CA member Gagan Thapa, Dr. Ganesh Mandal Nepali Congress youth leader Bishwaprakash Sharma, land rights activist Jagat Basnet and representatives of political parties, journalists and stakeholders were present.
